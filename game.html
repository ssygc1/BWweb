Black and White Game

Immerse yourself in the monochrome world of the Black and White Game.

Black & White is a cooperative puzzle game with a survival mode. Designed for two players, it combines strategic problem-solving and teamwork. Inspired by "Deru - The Art of Cooperation."

Puzzle Mode

Players control shapes of light and shadow, collaborating to solve puzzles. The levels increase in complexity, requiring strategic coordination to advance.

Survival Mode

In Survival Mode, players evade moving fluids, collect power-ups, and use tools to survive. Agile movements, quick thinking, and effective use of the environment are key to victory.
